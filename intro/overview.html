VoSeq overview¶

With VoSeq you can keep data on your voucher specimens and DNA sequences in one single place. Later on you can easily generate datasets ready for analysis in phylogenetic software that use file formats such as NEXUS, Phylip, TNT, MEGA and FASTA. With a couple of clicks, you can request datasets partitioned by gene or codon positions. You can also retrieve your dataset as aminoacid sequences or degenerated nucleotides.

Here is a quick overview on how to use VoSeq with your data.

  7. Upload voucher photos
  8. Create Excel table
  9. Update voucher

VoSeq comes with an administrative interface that allows you to create user accounts and upload, modify and delete voucher and sequence data. The user accounts are created by the administrator which can choose general or specific privileges for them. By default, users without accounts (guests) can only see voucher data but not sequences. They cannot create datasets, add or modify data.
